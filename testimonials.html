“Now our volunteers can look on their computer or phone to plan out their personal life with volunteering. They are able to look ahead to see our needs. Huge time saver and now we are in the year 2024!!! Our old school style worked but it wasn’t great especially for our younger volunteers who rely on everything to be electronic. BC (Breidigan Consulting) was so receptive to our needs. Their communication was stellar as was their ability to explain the system. We asked for something very simple and they delivered.”

It is an amazing time saver, convenience and organizational tool. BC diligently chose the right program that covered all our needs that we discussed and put it in place within the designated allotted time frame so it could be trialed prior to the end of the project. The schedule is easy to use and checks off all our needs.”
